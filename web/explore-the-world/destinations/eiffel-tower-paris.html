Frequently Asked Questions

What can I see in the virtual Eiffel Tower tour?

Our virtual tour offers stunning 360° views of Paris from different levels of the Eiffel Tower, detailed architectural exploration, and AI-guided insights about the tower's history and construction. You can explore the surrounding Champ de Mars, discover perfect photo spots, and learn about local attractions.

How accurate is the virtual Eiffel Tower experience?

Thanks to advanced WebGL and ThreeJS technology, combined with Mapbox's precise mapping data, our virtual recreation of the Eiffel Tower is highly detailed and accurate. While it's a virtual experience, it provides an authentic sense of scale, architecture, and surrounding environment.

Can I use this to plan my actual visit to the Eiffel Tower?

Absolutely! Many travelers use our virtual tour to plan their visits. The AI guide provides practical information about best visiting times, popular viewpoints, nearby attractions, and local tips. You can scout photography locations and learn about the area before your physical visit.
