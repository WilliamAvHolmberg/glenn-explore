Overview

Welcome to Glenn Explore - your gateway to endless adventures! Drive, fly, and explore a massive 3D world with friends or solo. Whether you're racing through city streets, cruising along coastal roads, or discovering hidden gems with AI-Glenn, the world is yours to explore. No objectives, no pressure - just pure exploration and good vibes.

Chat System

Connect with other explorers through the chat system

The chat system lets you connect with fellow explorers. Share discoveries, plan meetups, or just chat about your adventures. Click the chat bubble in the bottom-left corner to open the chat window.

Change Vehicle/Character

Choose your ride from various vehicles and characters

From sports cars to viking boats, and even a pepe frog - pick your perfect ride! Click the Settings button and select 'Change Model' to access the vehicle menu. Want your own model? Send us an image, and we'll turn it into a 3D model for just 2 USD!

Teleportation

Instantly travel to any location in the world

No destination is too far! Use the search bar to find any location, then choose between 'Navigate to' for a marked route or 'Teleport' for instant travel. Pro tip: Ask AI-Glenn for cool location suggestions!

Camera Controls

Master the camera controls for the perfect view

Take control of your view! Switch between 'Follow Car' and 'Free' camera modes, zoom in/out, and adjust the camera height. Use arrow keys or the camera controls in the settings panel to find your perfect angle.

Chat with AI-Glenn

Your AI travel companion

Meet AI-Glenn, your personal travel guide! Ask about beautiful places, get driving routes, or discover hidden gems. AI-Glenn can even teleport you directly to suggested locations - just click the teleport button in the responses!

Racing

Test your skills in time trials

Ready to race? Challenge yourself with time trials across various iconic locations. Beat other players' times and claim your spot on the leaderboard. Access races through the Racing button in the bottom menu.

Radio

Choose your driving soundtrack

Every adventure needs a soundtrack! Choose from multiple radio stations including Hip-Hop FM, Synthwave, Lo-Fi, and more. Control the volume and switch stations anytime through the radio panel.

Satellite Mode

Switch to satellite view for realistic terrain

Switch between standard and satellite views to experience the world differently. Perfect for exploring real landscapes and finding scenic routes. Toggle between modes in the Map Mode settings.
