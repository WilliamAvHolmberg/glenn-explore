April 11, 2024

Important Update: Temporary Access Fee 💰

Due to increasing Mapbox costs (potentially $1000+ monthly), we've had to implement a temporary onetime $1 access fee. This wasn't an easy decision, but it's necessary for sustainability. The past week has been incredible - we've seen amazing journeys like players driving from London to Kiev and walking from London to New Zealand!

We're actively exploring better monetization options to keep Glenn Explore accessible to everyone. Have ideas about sponsors, in-game purchases, or other solutions? Join our Discord and share your thoughts!
